Overview of our on-site facilities. Click a photo to open the gallery.

Goed Hoop Conference & Dining Room

Step aboard the Goed Hoop, where meetings and meals sail in style. Designed like a majestic ship with billowing sails and nautical details, this room blends adventure and elegance, inspiring conversation, collaboration, and celebration.

The Old Armoury Lounge

Where history meets comfort. Ancient swords line the walls, while plush seating and a cozy TV area invite relaxation and conversation. A perfect blend of inspiration and ease for every guest.

Forest Pool Retreat

A serene escape where a forest lapa, infinity pool, rustic bar, and sunlit solarium terrace come together. Relax, refresh, and let the beauty of nature surround you.

The Boma

Gather around the fire in this open-air sanctuary. With rustic seating, a glowing firepit, and the stars overhead, the Boma is perfect for storytelling, relaxation, and magical evenings under the night sky. A place where one can tell of the day's adventures or simply just relax.

The Little Cape Cottage

A charming Cape Dutch playhouse for children. With whimsical interiors, playful nooks, and a small garden, it’s a world designed for fun, creativity, and adventure.
