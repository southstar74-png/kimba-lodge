Explore & Experience

Adventure awaits at every turn—from open-vehicle safaris and walking trails to river escapes and unique wilderness experiences, choose your adventure!

Guided Game Drives

Set out across the savannah in an open safari vehicle as the landscape comes alive with movement and light. With an expert guide leading the way, you track wildlife through golden grasslands and along winding riverbanks, with the chance to encounter everything from graceful antelope to the iconic Big Five. Unobstructed views and the thrill of the open air make every sighting more vivid—whether it's a distant herd on the horizon or a close-up moment you'll never forget. It's a classic safari experience: immersive, dynamic, and full of surprises.

Morning Hike and Champagne Breakfast

At first light, the savannah awakens in a wash of golden hues as you set out on a guided walk along the Selati River. The air is cool and alive with birdsong, and each step reveals tracks, movement, and the quiet rhythm of the wild. Following the river's gentle curve, you arrive at a secluded clearing beneath acacia trees, where a beautifully prepared bush breakfast awaits. Fresh fruit, warm bread, and rich coffee are served with uninterrupted views of the landscape—an unforgettable start to the day in the heart of nature.

Snare Anti-Poaching Safari

Join a guided anti-poaching hike and step into the frontline of conservation. Accompanied by experienced rangers, you'll learn to spot and safely remove snares, read tracks and signs, and gain insight into the ongoing efforts to protect wildlife and their habitats.

As you move through the bush, you'll come to understand the real challenges faced in safeguarding these landscapes—and the impact of even small actions. It's a meaningful, hands-on experience that offers a deeper connection to the wild, where every step contributes to its future.

River Safari

Glide along the Olifants River on a peaceful safari, where the rhythm of the water reveals a different side of the wild. From the comfort of your boat, watch wildlife gather along the banks and spot birds and animals that thrive in this lush, hidden world—an intimate and refreshing perspective on the safari experience.

Kruger Park Visits

Discover Kruger National Park on a guided safari in an open vehicle, where every drive is shaped by the rhythms of the wild. Accompanied by expert guides, you'll track wildlife across vast plains, wooded areas, and riverbanks, with opportunities to encounter the Big Five and a rich variety of other species. With unobstructed views and insightful commentary, each sighting becomes more than just a moment—it's a deeper connection to one of Africa's most iconic landscapes. Half day and full day safaris are available.

Hans Merensky Golf Course

Play a round at the scenic Hans Merensky Golf Course, where the fairways blend seamlessly with the wild edges of the nearby bush. This unique course offers a relaxed yet memorable golfing experience in the heart of the lowveld. It's not uncommon to share the course with local wildlife—impala, warthog, and other game often wander across the greens, turning every round into something truly unforgettable. Surrounded by natural beauty and open skies, it's golf with a distinctly African twist.

Sunset Hike and Bushveld Braai

Set out on a guided sunset hike through the bushveld as the landscape softens into warm, golden light. As day turns to dusk, the sights and sounds of the wild shift around you, creating a calm yet immersive atmosphere.

The experience ends with a traditional bushveld braai under the open sky—freshly prepared food, a crackling fire, and the quiet magic of the evening making for a truly memorable close to the day.

Cultural Visits

Visit nearby communities and connect with local traditions through guided, respectful experiences. Discover traditional crafts, enjoy vibrant music, and taste authentic cuisine while gaining a deeper understanding of daily life and culture.

Helicopter Safari

Take to the skies on a breathtaking helicopter safari and experience the landscape from an entirely new perspective. As you lift above the bush, rivers, and open plains, sweeping views reveal the scale and beauty of the wilderness in every direction.

From the air, wildlife sightings take on a new magic—herds moving across the terrain, rivers winding like silver threads, and iconic landmarks unfolding beneath you. It's an unforgettable way to see the wild, full of perspective, contrast, and wonder.

Stories Written in the Stars

Spend an unforgettable evening under the African night sky at a guided astronomy experience with an expert astronomer. Far from city lights, the darkness reveals a brilliant canopy of stars stretching endlessly above the bush.

As you settle in, your guide brings the sky to life—pointing out constellations, planets, and celestial stories visible only in the southern hemisphere. It's a peaceful, awe-inspiring journey through the universe, set against the quiet sounds of the African night.
