Explore & Experience

From game drives to cultural visits — tailor your adventure.

Guided Game Drives

Experienced guides lead sunrise and sunset drives in search of wildlife.

Walking Safaris

Guided walks to experience tracks, plants and smaller desert-adapted wildlife.

Cultural Visits

Visit nearby communities to experience traditional crafts, music and local cuisine with respectful, guided interactions.

River Safari

Guided river safaris offering close encounters with riverine wildlife and birdlife — a peaceful way to experience nature up close.

Kruger Park Visits

Day trips and full-day excursions to Kruger National Park via the Phalaborwa gate — guided safaris and park transfers available.

Hans Merensky Golf Course

Play at the nearby Hans Merensky 18-hole championship course; we can arrange tee times and transfers on request.
