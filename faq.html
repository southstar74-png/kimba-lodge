Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a room at Kimba Lodge?
Use the 'Book Now' button on our Rooms or Contact page, or contact us via WhatsApp or phone for direct assistance.
What is your cancellation policy?
We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival. Please see our full policy on the booking page.
Do I need to pay a deposit?
Yes, a 50% deposit is required to secure your booking. The balance is due on arrival.

Check-in & Accommodation

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is from 2:00 PM. Check-out is by 10:00 AM. Early/late check-in may be available on request.
Are children welcome?
Yes, children of all ages are welcome. We have family-friendly rooms and facilities.
Is Wi-Fi available?
Yes, free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the lodge.

Facilities & Services

Is breakfast included?
Breakfast is available as an optional extra. Please request when booking.
Do you have a swimming pool?
Yes, guests have access to our outdoor swimming pool.
Is parking available?
Yes, secure on-site parking is provided free of charge.

Kruger Park & Location

How close are you to Kruger Park?
Kimba Lodge is less than 2km from the Phalaborwa Gate of Kruger National Park.
How far is the lodge from Hans Merensky golf course?
The golf course is 500m from the lodge.
Are guided safaris available?
We can arrange guided safaris and tours on request. Please ask at reception or when booking.
How do I get directions to Kimba Lodge?
See our Contact page for a map and directions, or use our address in your GPS: 33 Molengraafff Street, Phalaborwa.
What restaurants are nearby?
1) Villa Luso just 400m down the road.
2) Bushveld Terrace 1km away.

Policies & Practicalities

Is smoking allowed?
Smoking is not allowed inside rooms or indoor areas. Designated outdoor smoking areas are provided.
Are pets allowed?
Sorry, pets are not permitted at Kimba Lodge.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, EFT, and major credit/debit cards.
