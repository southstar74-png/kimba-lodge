Lion & Cubs Suite

The suite begins in the Cubs’ Room, a space alive with imagination and gentle joy. Soft colors, playful textures, and a sense of wonder fill the air, creating a sanctuary where young explorers can dream of wild adventures. But the true magic lies hidden in plain sight: a heavy, ornate wardrobe that stands as a silent sentinel against the wall.

Pushing past the soft fabrics inside, a secret passage reveals itself, leading you from the innocence of the Cubs’ Room into the regal splendor of the Lion Room. Here, the atmosphere shifts to one of quiet power and golden warmth. Earthy tones and rich finishes echo the majesty of the king of the savannah, offering a restful retreat that is both imposing and deeply comforting.

This interconnected haven is more than just a suite; it is a journey through a world of mystery and connection. Whether you are sharing stories by the soft glow of the lamp or discovering the "secret" link between rooms, the Lion & Cubs Suite invites you to embrace the bond of family and the thrill of discovery in a space designed for legends of all sizes.

Suite Details

Capacity: Up to 4 people

Includes:

  • Regal King bed in the Lion Room
  • Two comfortable beds in the Cubs Room
  • Secret interconnected wardrobe passage
  • Private lounge area and minibar
  • En-suite bathroom with premium amenities
  • Air conditioning and high-speed Wi-Fi
